What's this?


is a tool for more effective & inclusive assemblies.

Find out more about our team & our history here.

We want to package new & best practices into a simple, beautiful app that gets used in face-to-face meetings. We want to promote brevity & a more equal use of the right to speak (particularly across genders) with a traffic-light sand-timer countdown, synched to many screens, timeboxing turns in an ambient way that is almost auto-enforced.

We want to register and share the emotions & reactions of the group in real-time, create reports and, eventually, recordings of the events that are useful & interesting.

This project was prototyped during the Collective Intelligence for Democracy workshop at MediaLab Prado. It comes out of 3 years of experimentation at Wikipolitica.
